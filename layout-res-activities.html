“Independent lifestyle combining convenient services and personal support options with stunning nature views”

Life Enrichment

Chartwell Ridgepointe residents have big hearts, which is why our HOPE (Helping Others For Purposeful Engagement) program is so popular. When they aren’t knitting mittens for the homeless or bonnets for newborns, you’ll find them exercising, savouring a meal in our dining room and lounge, or using our unique outdoor putting green!


To further enhance residents’ retirement living experience, many of our most popular programs, activities and social events take place on-site in our modern amenities, where they can pursue hobbies, socialize with friends, or simply sit back and relax.

  • Billiards & Book club
  • Crafts & Day trips
  • Exercise/Fitness Facilities/Gym
  • Garden/Outdoor Space
  • Hobby/Art Studio
  • Library & piano
  • Movie theatre
  • Putting green
  • Residents’ computer room
  • Social and recreational programs
